Japan

From Buddhist Temples and Bullet Trains, to Sushi Bars and Video Games!
Japan (or Nihon, as locals call it) is a beautiful concoction of time-honoured tradition,
and a passionate drive towards technological advancement!

If you're interested in fantastic cultural heritage sites, Japan has you covered:
Mount Fuji, the Itsukushima Shinto Shrine, and the Horyu-ji Buddhist monuments (which are some of the oldest surving wooden structures in the world!)

Or if you're more interested in what modern Japan has to offer, the Ghibli Museum, Tokyo Skytree, and Nakano Broadway are must see stops on a Japanese vacation.

If all this seems a bit too much, a helpful first stop might be Shinjuku to see one of Tokyo's most well known tourism ambassadors: Godzilla!
